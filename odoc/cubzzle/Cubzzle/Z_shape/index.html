Module Cubzzle.Z_shape

type t

Defines the shape that must be assembled, from the heights (z axis) where the pieces start (bottom) and where they end (top). All proposed shapes are such that there is no hole in the middle of a z section, so this representation is suitable.

bottom and top are expected to index by y first -- such as in arr.(y).(x).
